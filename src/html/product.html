About The Bi-Fold Wallet

An ode to early American advertising wallets, this is our take on a traditional bi-fold with added room and easy access to the essentials. By far our largest format wallet, it features 4 card pockets and two hidden pockets as well as a large, easily accessible, cash flap.

Materials
  • Wickett & Craig English Bridle Leather (USA)
  • Tex 90 Bonded Nylon Thread (USA)
  • Solid Brass Snaps (USA)
Construction Details

  • Hand stitched reinforcements
  • High stitch count for a refined and sturdy construction
