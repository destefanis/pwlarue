About P.W. Larue Co.

The P.W. LaRue Co. is a design and manufacturing studio based in Chicago, Illinois. We specialize in handcrafted leather wallets and canvas bags. Our motive is to create long lasting products by using the highest quality materials. This, in unison with thoughtful construction techniques results in products we believe will endure the test of time.
